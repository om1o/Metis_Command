## Feature blocks

### 1. Your thoughts stay yours
Metis never sends your data to a cloud. Every conversation, every file, every idea is processed on your own hardware — behind your own firewall.

### 2. It remembers you
After a week, Metis recognizes how you talk. After a month, it anticipates what you need. Your personal assistant, trained exclusively on you.

### 3. It writes its own tools
Ask Metis to do something new. It writes the code, runs it in a sealed vault, and adds the skill to its memory — forever.

### 4. Five experts, one window
Metis orchestrates a team of specialized AI agents: one for code, one for research, one for deep reasoning, one for long documents. You speak to one interface; five minds respond.

### 5. Always on, never in the way
A single keyboard shortcut brings Metis forward. Another makes it disappear. It lives quietly in the tray until you need it.
