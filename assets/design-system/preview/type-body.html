Component rules

  • Sidebar nav: 14/1.2/500. Active 650.
  • Chat — user: 15/1.55/500 · assistant: 15/1.6/450.

Accessibility

  • AA contrast on all text: body ≥4.5:1, large ≥3:1.
  • Min size 13px. No 11–12px outside eyebrows/dot-labels.
  • No thin weights (≤300) anywhere — light bg burns them out.
  • Focus: 3px --shadow-focus ring on all controls.
  • Tracking: body 0em · labels/buttons +0.01em only.
